# RentFAX Support Workflow

## Purpose:

To define how customer issues are triaged, resolved, and documented.

---

## Level 1: Dashboard Support

Handled by RentFAX Support Reps

* Password resets
* Report creation help
* Dashboard navigation

## Level 2: Technical or Legal Issues

Escalated to:

* Engineering team (if technical)
* Compliance/legal partner (if legal inquiry)

## Level 3: Account Review

Super Admins only:

* Access audit logs
* Manage fraud signals
* Approve/ban partner companies
